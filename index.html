Defeat Megatron!

How to Play:


Megatron will give a random number at the start of each game.

There are four Transformers below. By clicking on one of the Transformers, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You can defeat Megatron, and win, by matching your total score to Megatron's random number. But you lose the game if your total score goes above Megatron's random number.

The value of each Transformer is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of each Transformer and Megatron will change his random number.
